Construction!

Construction was absolutely fantastic this decade! The greatest accomplishment being that every city built a wall, at least a wooden one. This extra protection against enemies is a welcome one! In addition to the walls, the authorities have also constructed trading stations throughout the Seleucid Empire in various places. Some of these being Damascus and Antioch. Numerous Barracks have been constructed as well, most likely to rise up to the sudden demand for better soldiers and more recruitment during this decade. However, the people have noted that the most important construction done was the roads being built throughout the Empire and the farms! This was a great decade for construction in the glorious Seleucid Empire!
