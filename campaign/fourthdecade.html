Decade IV

Imperial News!

Thus comes an end the bloody four decades! The Royal family wants to announce that Kassandros has finally come of age to wage battle. Not a moment too soon either! Our great military continues to conquer lands and peoples! Soon there will not be a single part of the Seleucid Empire that the sun sets or does not belong to the Empire!Onwards to Thebes! Onwards to Greece! Onwards to Rome! When Rome falls, there is nothing left that could possibly offer a challenge to our great civilization! Soon our elephants and chariots will be roaming the countrysides of every providence under the sun!

Construction!

The construction goes well this Decade, just like the decades before! A sure sign of the greatness of the Seleucid Empire! Besdies the ports and various repairs done to captured cities, the great city of Selecuia has built calvary stables which have enabled the Empire to recruit Elephants! It is very good that at least a significant amount of the money is going to buildnig great buildings and not just to the upkeep of the military!

Diplomacy!

Diplomacy is once again all over the place. The Empire started the decade at war with nobody but Pontus which was came as a suprise. But that peace came to an end when we attacked the city of Mazaka in the north thus angering the Pontic people more. Eygpt was next when Anaksarxos attacked the city of Memphis. No peace for the Egyptians lest they rebuild their armies! The Greek Cities have declared war on the Empire! Our diplomats are extrodinary individuals to be so successful in their negotiations!
