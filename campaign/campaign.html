Briefing:

This is my website about my campaign to take over as much of the ancient world as I can in 40 turns. I am playing the Seleucid Empire in the Rome: Total War game. This game is actually very fun to play and has a lot of small details to it that I find more of each time I play. As I play I am able to fight countless battles against various Factions, I will be sharing my videos on this website in the military sections of the Decades. If I do not just want to fight I can also focus on other aspects of the game which I am doing in this website. Just to note, I am going by four decades for just 40 turns, years are more like 4 turns so my decades are not exactly decades in the game. I am following the Faction family, trying to keep my citizens happy, protect my borders, and be as diplomatic as I can before I betray my allies and pillage their lands!
